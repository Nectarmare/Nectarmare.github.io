The Game

Controlling a small honey bee the player of this game will collect nectar while avoiding numerous perilous dangers on the way. Inclduing but not limited to the carnivouros fly, the skullspider and the evil flower with an eye resting in the middle. The game takes place in a number of beautiful evokative environments ranging from deep forest to summery fields and tranquil water.
